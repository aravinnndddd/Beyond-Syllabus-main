# OECST725: Mobile Application Development  

## Course Objectives

1. To impart comprehensive mobile development knowledge  
2. To provide proficiency in Flutter & Dart, UI/UX design skills  
3. To present industry practices and deployment  

---

## Syllabus Modules

### Module 1: Fundamentals
- Mobile platforms – iOS, Android  
- Flutter – history, features, benefits, environment setup  
- App architectures – MVC, MVVM, BLoC; Dart basics  

### Module 2: UI/UX Design
- Mobile UI/UX principles  
- Responsive UIs with Flutter widgets – Stateless & Stateful  
- Layouts – Container, Column, Row, Stack; navigation & routing  
- Themes & styles  

### Module 3: Advanced Flutter
- State management – Provider, Riverpod, BLoC  
- Networking – HTTP, JSON parsing, RESTful APIs  
- Data persistence – SQLite, SharedPreferences, Hive  
- Asynchronous programming – Futures, async/await, Streams  

### Module 4: Industry Practices & Deployment
- Advanced UI components & animations  
- App security best practices, testing & debugging  
- Publishing to Google Play & Apple App Store  
- Trends & future of Flutter development
